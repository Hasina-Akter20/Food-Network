About Me

This is Hasina Akter. I am a chef, host and food stylist residing in LA. I started her career as a pastry chef in Santa Monica while attending culinary school and from there began working with The Food Network, Cooking Channel and ABC as a food stylist. I have hosted various digital series with Tastemade, Thrive Market, ABC Go and Yahoo! that showcase my approachable, healthy and easy style of cooking.
